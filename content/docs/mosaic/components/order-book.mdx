---
title: OrderBook
description: Render an already-synchronized order-book snapshot with selectable price levels.
---

`OrderBook` renders a complete Mosaic `OrderBookSnapshot`. It does not open a
socket, fetch a snapshot, or apply updates internally.

```tsx
<OrderBook
  snapshot={book}
  quoteCurrency="USD"
  layout="stacked"
  depth={20}
  showTotals
  onSelectPrice={(price) => tradeDraft.setLimitPrice(price)}
/>
```

## Rendering behavior

- `depth` limits visible levels per side and defaults to 15.
- `layout` accepts `stacked` or `split`.
- `showTotals` controls cumulative quantity display.
- asks render above the spread and bids below it in the stacked layout.
- depth bars are calculated from cumulative quantities.

When `onSelectPrice` is present, levels become accessible price-selection
buttons and report `bid` or `ask` through the callback.

## Maintaining the snapshot

Validate incoming snapshots and updates with Mosaic core, then maintain the
current snapshot with `applyOrderBookUpdate`. On identity mismatch or lost
sequence continuity, stop applying updates and obtain a fresh snapshot.

For a Relay-backed flow, see [Relay to Mosaic](/docs/guides/relay-to-mosaic).
